1. Who we are

Fixins is a meal planning and grocery support application operated by Aerion Health and built by Simple Digital Solution LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use Fixins.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated by your use of the service, and limited technical information needed for security and reliability.

  • Account information: email address, profile or display name, account preferences, and authentication data.
  • Content you create: meal plans, shopping list entries, pantry-related inputs, recipe interactions, and similar planning content.
  • Device and usage information: app version, request metadata, log events, and diagnostic data used for operations and abuse prevention.
  • Location-related information: approximate location signals to show nearby stores and local savings context when location features are enabled.

3. Permissions and sensitive data use

Fixins requests access only when a feature needs it.

  • Location (when in use): used to show nearby grocery options and improve shopping relevance.
  • Camera and photos (if enabled in your build): used for receipt or pantry image capture when you choose those features.
  • Notifications (if enabled): used for reminders and app updates that you can manage in device settings.

You can control permissions in your device settings at any time.

4. How we use information

  • Provide and operate core app functionality.
  • Authenticate users and protect account security.
  • Generate meal planning, shopping, and cooking support experiences.
  • Maintain performance, troubleshoot reliability issues, and prevent abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell personal information, including email addresses.

5. Service providers and infrastructure

We use service providers that process data on our behalf for infrastructure and operational needs, including:

  • Microsoft Azure (application hosting, database, and secret management).
  • Authentication and identity providers you choose (for example, Apple or Google sign-in).
  • Email and communications providers for transactional account messages.
  • Cloud AI or OCR services used for receipt and pantry image workflows when those features are used.

These providers are bound by contractual and security controls appropriate to their role.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

  • Account and profile data: retained while the account is active and for a limited period after deletion as required by law or security needs.
  • Operational logs and diagnostics: generally retained for up to 12 months, unless a longer period is needed for legal, fraud, or security investigations.
  • User-generated planning content: retained until deletion by the user or account closure, subject to backup and legal retention windows.

7. Your rights and choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information.

  • In-app account deletion is available in Profile Settings.
  • Authenticated data export is available through the profile export service.
  • You may submit privacy requests by email using the contact details below.

We respond to verified requests within applicable legal timelines.

8. Children

Fixins is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without required parental consent.

9. Security

We use technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including access controls, encrypted transport, and managed secret handling.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by an updated date and, where required, additional notice.
