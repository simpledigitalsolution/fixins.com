Delete Your Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your Fixins account and associated personal data.

Back to Fixins Privacy Policy Email Support

Before you delete

Account deletion is permanent. Once completed, your account, meal plans, shopping lists, pantry data, and profile information will be removed and cannot be recovered.

Delete from inside the app

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside Fixins:

1
Open the app and sign in

Make sure you are signed into the account you want to delete.

2
Go to Profile

Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner of any screen.

3
Open Settings

Tap Settings from the profile menu.

4
Tap "Delete account"

Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap Delete account. You will be asked to confirm. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email. Send your request to:

admin@simpledigitalsolution.com

Include the email address associated with your Fixins account. We will process your deletion request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Profile and display name
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Pantry and receipt data
  • Household membership and preferences
  • Usage history and session data

Some anonymized, non-identifiable aggregate data may be retained for operational and legal purposes as described in our Privacy Policy.
